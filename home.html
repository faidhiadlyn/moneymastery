Dashboard

1. Calendar Integration
This acts as an indicator that shows users when important financial dates are coming up, like investment reviews, bill payments, and savings targets. The ability to manage one's finances and remain organized is facilitated by this feature.

2. Effortless Navigation to Features
The dashboard provides easy access to all financial management features. Navigable entry points guarantee a user-friendly experience whether Expense Tracking, Goal and Planning, or the Investment Module. To explore in-depth features and insights, users only need to click on the appropriate available features.

3. Goal and Planning Progress Bar
A graphic depiction of the user's progress towards their financial objectives. By making it simple for users to keep track of their accomplishments and milestones, this progress bar acts as a motivating tool. A brief summary of their financial development is provided by the visual indicator.

4. Logout button
With a focus on user control and security, the logout button makes sure users can safely end sessions when needed. Customers' financial information is further safeguarded by this feature, which enables them to log out of their MoneyMastery account. It gives users control over their privacy and account access, improving the platform's overall security.


Features

1. Expense Tracking
With this feature, users can keep a close eye on their expenses. It offers a thorough analysis of costs for all kinds of expenses, including groceries, bills, entertainment, and so forth.

Users can use interactive graphs and reports to input, classify, and monitor their spending patterns over time. By providing information about where money is being spent, expense tracking aids in budget management and empowers users to make wise financial decisions.

2. Goal and Planning
The management and setting of financial goals are made easier by this section. It is possible for users to list both short- and long-term goals, such as retirement planning, house buying, or vacation savings.

Setting realistic goals, assigning budgets, and monitoring the progress made towards these objectives are made easier by this feature. It contains timetable makers, savings plan creators, and alert systems for when goals are met.

3. Investment Module
Giving users useful advice and instructional resources to help them make educated investment decisions is the main goal of this section. It provides several tools, including tutorials, articles, and guides, that break down investment concepts, asset classes, risk management techniques, and the fundamentals of portfolio construction.

With an emphasis on succinct, clear information supported by examples and doable steps, it helps users understand investment concepts and apply them to actual situations. With the help of this module, users should be able to confidently navigate the investing landscape.
